Agile Process Spitballer

An application designed to help developers spark ideas during the agile process

Agile Process Spitballer

This app stemmed from a personal need during my 2nd hackathon. Our team was spitballing random words to help spark ideas to fit a certain criteria.

Spitballing can take a long time so my team decided to make a spitballer itself. This could be useful for anyone who needs to get their creative juices flowing.

We used AJAX to call 4 different API's (Random Words, Word Association, iTunes, Pixabay) to cohesively bring together something that would assist the creation process.

  • TECHNOLOGIESJavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, OOP format, JSDOC Documentation
  • AUDIENCECreators/Developers
  • LIVE WEBSITEhttps://www.agilespitballer.vivianhdle.com/
  • GITHUB https://www.github.com/vivianhdle/agile_process_spitballer
